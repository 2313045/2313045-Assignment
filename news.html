Oleksandr Usyk all set for the Undisputed title against the Gypsy King Tyson Fury

Oleksandr Usyk vs Tyson Fury is set May 18th. This highly anticipated matchup has been hyped up the last couple of years.

Tyson Fury, the towering British boxer known for his exceptional movement and unorthodox tactics, holds the WBC heavyweight title and is known for his heart and unpredictability in the ring.

On the other hand, Usyk, the Ukrainian master technician and former undisputed cruiserweight champion, brings unparalleled boxing IQ and slick footwork to the table.

With both fighters possessing unique skill sets and unyielding determination, this matchup has got everyone on their toes eagerly awaiting to see who will be the Undisputed World Heavyweight Champion in 20 years since the renowned Lennox Lewis won it back in 2003

Artur Beterbiev vs Dimtri Bivol announced for June 2024

Artur Beterbiev vs Dimtri Bivol will take place in the summer this year for June.

Matchroom promotor Eddie Hearn and Queensbury promotor have agreed to do a head to head show in June have five of their boxers face each other to prove whos got the best promotion in the UK.

Beterbiev, known for his devastating punching power and relentless aggression, holds the unified WBC and IBF titles, while Bivol, with his slick boxing skills and technical prowess, is the reigning WBA champion. Frazer Clarke vs Fabio Wardley close fight draw

Frazer Clark vs Fabio Wardley has just been and it was a slugfest.

Going in to the fight was Frazer Clarke, the British super heavyweight prospect and former Commonwealth Games gold medalist, brings a formidable mix of power and agility into the ring, coupled with his extensive amateur pedigree.

And there is Fabio Wardley, known for his explosive punching and relentless aggression, has swiftly climbed the ranks of the heavyweight division with an impressive unbeaten record.

Wardley knocked down Clarke in the 5th round. while Clarke bloodied Wardley's nose in this epic show down, the result was a draw
