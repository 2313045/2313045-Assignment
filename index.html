The Kings of the Divisions

Heavyweight

Oleksandr Usyk

Oleksandr Usyk is the current Unified World Heavyweight Champion holding the WBA, IBF, WBO, and Ring Magazine championships.

His upcoming fight is for the Undisputed World Heavyweight Championship where he will be facing WBC and Lineal World Heavyweight Champion Tyson Fury.

Light Heavyweight

Artur Beterbiev

Artur Beterbiev is the current Unified World Light-Heavyweight Champion the WBA, IBF, WBO, and Ring Magazine championships.

His upcoming fight is for the Undisputed World Light-Heavyweight Championship where he will be facing WBC and Lineal World Heavyweight Champion Dimiti Bivol.

Middleweight

Jermall Charlo

Jermall Charlo is the current WBC Middleweight Champion of the world

He has no upcoming fights at the moment so we will keep updated once there is any fight news

Welterweight

Terrance Crawford

Terrance Crawford is the current Undisputed Welterweight Champion of the world and pound of pound best boxer on the planet.

He has no upcoming fights at the moment so we will keep updated once there is any fight news

lightweight

Gervonta Davis

Gervonta is the current WBA Lightweight Champion of the world

He is currently negotiating with Frank Martin a Lightweight contender for a fight.

Featherweight

Leigh Wood

Leigh Wood is the current WBA Featherweight Champion of the world

He is currently negotiating a rematch with contender and former champion Josh Warrington who he knocked out in his last fight.
